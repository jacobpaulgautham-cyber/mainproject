THE HANDLOOM MARK

The Handloom Mark is a certification by the Government of India that guarantees a textile was made entirely on a handloom. Every product from Eravathody carries this mark. It is not a brand, it is a guarantee of process, of human labour, and of a tradition that has no machine equivalent.
